HOW TO CONQUER SUBSTANCE ABUSE AND ADDICTION

1.Wait to Respond

When something stressful happens to you, take a moment to breathe deeply and calm your mind. This is so that you can address the situation with level-headedness.

2.Mindfulness and Meditation

Use mindfulness and meditation techniques to work through emotions. This allows you to focus on the present moment, observe our internal experiences and to accept them without judgement or negativity.

3.Keep Busy

This is the time to do the things you've been putting off. Adapt a behaviour of keeping to-do-lists.

4.Stay Healthy

Treat yourself to nutritious, satisfying meals and nourish your body with vitamins. Drink plenty of water too.

5.Exercise

Exercise is a great way to relieve anxiety and stress due to the endorphins released during physical activity.It also helps get rid of the toxins caused by substance abuse.

6.Journal

Writing can be a great way to release and work out any thoughts that you are struggling with.
